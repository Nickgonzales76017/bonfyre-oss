Sales calls → pricing surfaces.

A sales call is already most of the context you need to build a proposal. Bonfyre takes that recording and runs it through: transcription, brief, entity extraction, offer generation, payment link, metered follow-up, and a ledger entry — the full revenue pipeline from conversation to close.

Worked example

A discovery call → structured brief → priced proposal → ledger entry.

The scenario: a 30-minute discovery call is recorded. A prospect has described their needs, mentioned a budget range, and been asked 5 qualifying questions. Here's what runs next.

Step 1 — BonfyreIngest

Call recording normalised and fingerprinted

BonfyreIngest pulls the recording, normalises loudness, resamples to 16 kHz mono, trims silence. BLAKE2b hash stored. If the same call file is uploaded twice — from a video platform and a local export — only one version is processed.

Step 2 — BonfyreTranscribe

30-minute discovery call → transcript in under 2 minutes

Local Whisper processes the call at 15× real-time. A 30-minute call produces a transcript in under 2 minutes. The transcript stays on your machine — it doesn't pass through a third-party transcription API.

Step 3 — BonfyreSegment + BonfyreTranscriptClean

Your rep and the prospect labelled separately; fillers removed

Speaker diarisation separates rep from prospect. BonfyreTranscriptClean removes hallucinations and repeated fillers. Prospect statements — the ones that matter for proposal generation — are now cleanly separated from rep questions.

Step 4 — BonfyreBrief

Structured sales brief: pain, budget, timeline, objections

BonfyreBrief extracts a structured brief from the transcript: primary pain points stated by the prospect, budget signals mentioned, decision timeline, stakeholders named, objections raised, and recommended next steps. CRM-ready in structured JSON — no manual data entry.

Step 5 — BonfyreTag + BonfyreEmbed

Prospect's company, stack, and problems tagged and vectorised

BonfyreTag extracts named entities: company name, tech stack mentioned, competitors referenced, use-case categories. BonfyreEmbed vectorises the brief. This embedding is what lets you ask "find all prospects with similar pain to ACME Corp" and get ranked results across your entire call archive.

Step 6 — BonfyreOffer

Proposed package assembled from brief context

BonfyreOffer generates a structured proposal from the brief: service package, delivery timeline, pricing tiers based on the budget signals stated in the call, and an expiry date. The output is a proposal object — not a document, but a machine-readable structure that downstream steps can price, attach payment to, and track in the ledger.

Step 7 — BonfyreFinance + BonfyrePay

Proposal priced and payment link generated

BonfyreFinance applies pricing rules to the offer: volume discounts, payment terms, tax-inclusive amounts. BonfyrePay generates a checkout session. The prospect receives a link to a payment page — no manual Stripe dashboard work, no forwarding the brief to a separate billing team.

Step 8 — BonfyreMeter + BonfyreOutreach

Follow-up cadence activated; usage tracked from day one

BonfyreMeter activates metered tracking on the account — if the offer includes usage-based pricing, the meter starts at proposal acceptance. BonfyreOutreach schedules follow-up touches: a 48-hour no-response ping, a 7-day check-in, and a 14-day expiry reminder — all triggered from the proposal issue date without calendar work.

Step 9 — BonfyreLedger + BonfyreSync

Deal recorded in the ledger; CRM synced

On acceptance, BonfyreLedger records the transaction: deal value, payment terms, associated account, source call reference. BonfyreSync pushes the ledger entry and brief to your internal CRM or data store. The pipeline from "recorded discovery call" to "CRM deal entry" requires zero manual steps.

One 30-minute discovery call → structured brief, priced proposal, payment link, follow-up cadence, and ledger entry. Processed in under 5 minutes.
