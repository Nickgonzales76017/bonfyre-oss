# Concept: Packaging

## Summary
Packaging is the framing layer that turns raw capability into an understandable offer.

## Why It Matters
- buyers purchase outcomes, not internal complexity
- good packaging raises perceived value without changing the core work

## Where It Applies
- offer writing
- bundled services
- naming and positioning
