# Concept: Decentralization

## Summary
Decentralization distributes control, storage, or execution across multiple nodes instead of relying on one central platform.

## Why It Matters
- it can reduce dependency and infrastructure cost
- it matters for peer-to-peer or local-first ideas in the vault

## Where It Applies
- torrent-based distribution
- browser-based compute
- local node networks
