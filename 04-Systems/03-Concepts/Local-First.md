# Concept: Local-First

## Summary
Local-first means doing work on your own machine or inside your own files whenever that is cheaper, more private, and good enough.

## Why It Matters
- lowers recurring cost
- reduces platform dependence
- keeps sensitive work closer to you

## Where It Applies
- local transcription
- file-based workflows
- lightweight personal infrastructure
