# Concept: Personal Agency

## Summary
Personal agency is the ability to convert your own time, tools, and judgment into real outcomes without waiting for permission.

## Why It Matters
- it keeps the system biased toward execution
- it turns the vault into an active operating layer instead of passive storage

## Where It Applies
- deciding quickly
- testing offers
- building micro-businesses from existing assets
