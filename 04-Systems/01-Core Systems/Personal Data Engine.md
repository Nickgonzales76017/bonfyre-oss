# System: Personal Data Engine

## Purpose
Capture what happens inside the vault, extract patterns from it, and feed those patterns back into better execution and monetization decisions.

## Outcome
- value created: memory, pattern recognition, and sharper prioritization
- customer: you and every other system in the vault
- measurable result: better weekly focus and fewer repeated mistakes

## Core Mechanism
Daily logs, project updates, offer tests, and outcomes become structured signals that guide what to double down on and what to stop.

## Tooling
- operating tools: daily logs, weekly reviews, structured project and offer notes
- automation tools: review helpers, future note summarization, pattern extraction workflows
- supporting infrastructure: templates, folder structure, linked concept notes
- meta tooling note:
  - [[04-Systems/04-Meta/Tooling Stack]]
  - [[04-Systems/04-Meta/Vault Operating System]]

## Inputs
- trigger: work completed, money generated, friction encountered
- raw materials: daily logs, project notes, offer notes, results
- tools: Obsidian, structured templates, reviews

## Outputs
- artifact: weekly and eventually monthly summaries
- decision: what to focus on, kill, or refine
- downstream effect: smarter projects, offers, and distribution choices

## Flow
1. capture work and outcomes in logs
2. review patterns across projects and offers
3. feed the best signals back into planning and prioritization

## Control Layer
- owner: you
- quality check: logs must record concrete outputs, not vague intentions
- manual override: prioritize observed results over theory

## Current State
- the idea exists and the daily log folder is live
- logging structure is now stronger than before
- the review loop still needs actual repeated use

## Bottlenecks
- not enough historical data yet
- requires consistency to become useful
- there is no weekly review history yet

## Metrics
- throughput: number of days logged
- quality: usefulness of reviews for future decisions
- revenue or time saved: reduced wasted effort on low-signal projects

## Next Improvement
👉 use the daily log template consistently and create the first weekly review after a few execution days
