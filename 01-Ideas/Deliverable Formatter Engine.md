# Idea: Deliverable Formatter Engine

## Summary
Build a formatter that turns transcripts and summaries into buyer-ready markdown, PDF, email, or exportable note packages.

## Core Insight
Perceived quality often comes from formatting and delivery, not just transcript accuracy.

## First Use Case
Output a polished markdown deliverable with sections for transcript, summary, action items, and key decisions.

## Why It Might Work
- inefficiency: raw artifacts feel unfinished
- arbitrage: better packaging increases trust and price
- trend: productized service delivery beats ad hoc file dumps
