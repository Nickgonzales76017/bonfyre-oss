# Idea: AI + Overseas Labor Pipeline

## Summary
A hybrid system that combines local AI processing with low-cost overseas labor to deliver scalable, high-margin services.

---

## Core Insight
Pure automation is not enough. Pure labor is inefficient.

The highest leverage comes from:
→ AI handling 80%  
→ humans handling edge cases  

This creates a cost-efficient, scalable pipeline.

---

## Philosophy
- automate first, outsource second
- AI for speed, humans for quality
- systems over individual work
- margins through structure, not effort

---

## Why it Works
- inefficiency: high domestic labor costs
- arbitrage: global wage differences
- trend: hybrid AI + human workflows

---

## Inputs
- AI tools:
  - local models (llama.cpp, Whisper)
  - optional cloud AI
- overseas workers (Upwork, direct hires)
- defined workflows and SOPs
- task queue (simple system or manual)

---

## Output
- processed data
- content (edited, cleaned, formatted)
- completed services
- scalable workflows

---

## Execution Path
1. Define a service workflow (e.g. transcription → summary)
2. Automate first pass with AI
3. Identify failure / edge cases
4. Route those to overseas workers
5. Combine outputs into final deliverable
6. Deliver to customer

---

## First Use Case
Local AI Transcription Service:
- AI generates transcript + summary
- human reviews + cleans output ($1–$3 cost)
- sell for $10–$20
- maintain margin while improving quality

---

## Monetization
- service fees
- scaled service contracts
- bundled services
- higher-tier “human-reviewed” pricing

---

## Constraints
- communication overhead
- quality control
- requires clear SOPs
- dependency on worker reliability

---

## Leverage Type
- labor (cheap + scalable)
- automation (AI preprocessing)
- systems (repeatable workflows)
