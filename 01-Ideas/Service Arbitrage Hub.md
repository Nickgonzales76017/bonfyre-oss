# Idea: Service Arbitrage Hub

## Summary
A coordination layer that sells services (pet care, cleaning, delivery) and outsources execution to cheaper providers.

## Why it Works
- inefficiency: fragmented local service markets
- arbitrage: price differences between providers
- trend: increasing demand for convenience

## Inputs
- tools:
  - website
  - scheduling system
- skills:
  - sales
  - coordination
- people:
  - contractors (local or overseas)

## Output
- completed services
- managed experiences

## Execution Path
1. Pick niche (e.g. dog walking)
2. Build simple landing page
3. Collect leads
4. Route jobs to providers
5. manage quality

## Monetization
- margin per job
- subscription services

## Constraints
- trust
- quality control

## Leverage Type
- labor
- coordination
