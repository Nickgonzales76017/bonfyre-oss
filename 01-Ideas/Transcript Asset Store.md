# Idea: Transcript Asset Store

## Summary
Create a simple storage and retrieval layer for transcripts, summaries, and deliverables so completed jobs become reusable assets instead of one-off files.

## Core Insight
Past transcripts are not just outputs. They are reusable data, proof, and training material for better future systems.

## First Use Case
Save each job with metadata, transcript, summary, action items, and buyer context in a structured folder or index.

## Why It Might Work
- inefficiency: completed outputs are easy to lose in ad hoc folders
- arbitrage: structured history improves search, reuse, and quality tuning
- trend: file-based asset stores work well for small operators
